Privacy policy

Draft. Last updated July 21, 2026. Pending legal review.

This page explains, in plain language, what information this website collects and what we do with it. "We" means IturanMob USA.

What we collect

The only personal information this site collects is what you type into our contact forms (demo request, pilot start, or project meeting):

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • Fleet size (optional)
  • Primary use case (optional)

If you never submit a form, we collect no personal information from you on this site.

Why we collect it

For one purpose: so our sales team can contact you about mob-connect. That's what you're asking for when you submit a form, and that's what we use it for.

What we don't do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Cookies and analytics

This site may use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the page (for example, which sections are read and which buttons are clicked). When enabled, Google Analytics sets cookies and collects usage data under Google's own policies. We use this data in aggregate only: to improve the site, not to identify you.

If you start a pilot order, we store the details you typed into the form in a short-lived cookie (30 minutes) on our own domain, so our order page can pre-fill your checkout. That cookie is first-party only and is never shared with third parties.

Deleting your information

Want your information removed? Email us at salesmob@ituranusa.com and we will delete it.
